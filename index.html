About Zine

ZINE INNOVATIONS is South African company comprised of a highly skilled international team of scientists, engineers and biologists.
Our work based on introducing modern technology, efficient use of proven methods and sustainable development.

What we do

MICROBIOLOGICAL SOLUTIONS

Highly equipped research laboratories . Microcloning and plant adaptation. Scientific methods providing solutions and analysis of the agriculture sector business model.

AGRITECH SOLUTIONS

Energy efficiency improvements. Engineers and IT specialists. An introduction of organic automated technology with remote control systems.

DEVELOPMENT AND RESEARCHING

Advanced engineering development services for Innovative Agrarian Complexes, Smart GreenHouse methods and Vertical Farming
