About Zine

ZINE INNOVATIONS is South African company comprised of a highly skilled international team of scientists, engineers and biologists.
Our work based on introducing modern technology, efficient use of proven methods and sustainable development.

What we do

ANALYTICS & SOLUTIONS

Solutions and Analysis of agrarian business problems. Highly equipped research and technological laboratories. Plant adaptation. Energy efficiency improvements. Consulting from biologists, engineers and IT specialists.

DEVELOPMENT

Engineering development services for Innovative Agrarian Complexes, Smart GreenHouse methods and Vertical Farming. An introduction of automation with remote control systems.

STARTUP SUPPORT

Assistance in the realisation of innovative projects in the agrarian sector of Africa. Educative, training programs, professional support and International collaboration.
